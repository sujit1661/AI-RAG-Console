How to use RAGCORE

Quick start

1

Create a Workspace

Click + New Workspace. Each workspace keeps documents and chats separate.

2

Upload Documents

Click Upload Document. Supports PDF, Word, Excel, images. Max 50MB.

3

Ask Questions

Type in the chat box. The AI answers only from your documents. Ask for summaries, facts, or comparisons.

4

Manage Chats & Files

Create multiple chats per workspace. Hover workspaces to rename or delete. Delete files from the Documents panel.

Tips

  • • Upload multiple documents — AI searches across all of them
  • • Ask follow-up questions — AI remembers the conversation
  • • Page numbers below answers show where info came from
  • • Use 👍 / 👎 on answers to give feedback
